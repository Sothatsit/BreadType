How to create a quiz

1. Set the title of your quiz (e.g What type of bread are you?)


2. Write four bread types (or wine, cheese, or book types!) that will correspond to the four possible categories people will be placed in based on the answers they submit.


3. Create your questions by first selecting the style of question from a multiple choice question, a continuous slider, or a discrete slider. The options available, or the min and max values for the question can now be configured below the type selector.


4. Add more questions by hitting the "Add Question" button, and repeat step 3 to configure them all as you like!


5. Submit your quiz when you have finished creating questions.
